About Me

As a Social Entrepreneur and Product Designer, I am always looking to come up with the most human centric solutions. I use a variety of research and analysis tools and techniques to really get an in-depth understanding of the problem and use my diverse experience to come up with the most optimal solution.

I have twelve years of international experience working for top employers such as TD Bank Financial Group, Canada, York University – Institute for Research and Innovation in Sustainability (IRIS) and IMMAP (United Nations and USAID funded project). I have served as a Founding Member on Board of Directors for IT Park Kohat (Pakistan) and Arbitrage Magazine (Toronto) and work as a Research Assistant at RRU - Directorate of Projects, United Nations Development Program and Ministry of SAFRON, Government of Pakistan.
